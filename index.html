HELBUG

A BUG in the Capital region of Finland. Capital of Finlang being Helsinki, naturally our BUG will be named HELBUG.

HELBUGs goal is to promote the use of the BSD variants and the knowledge relating to this. Helsinki area BSD User Groups (HELBUGs) plan is to have semiregular meetings with varying agendas. These will include talks, seminars, hacking workshops and general discussion on BSD variants.

First meetup

Preliminary agenda is to host a few talks by volunteers and decide on the locations and the interval of our BUG will have meetings. More detailed agenda and time for the first meeting will be posted later to this website.
