A BUG in the Capital region of Finland.

Capital of Finland being Helsinki, naturally our BUG is be named HELBUG.

HELBUGs goal is to promote the use of the BSD variants and the knowledge relating to this.

Helsinki area BSD User Groups (HELBUGs) plan is to have semiregular meetings with varying agendas.

These will include talks, seminars, hacking workshops and general discussion on BSD variants.

BUGs agenda

Agenda is to host talks by volunteers and learn on all things BSD.

More detailed agenda and time for the second meeting will be posted later to this website or the meetup website.
